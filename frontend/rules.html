How to Play:

  1. Start a Game: Click "LAUNCH A GAME!" to create a new game and share the code with a friend, or click "JOIN A GAME!" to enter a code.
  2. Build Your Deck: Choose 10 cards from Rock, Paper, and Scissors. You can select manually or get a random deck. Powers are automatically assigned!
  3. Play Cards: Each turn, select a card from your hand and play it against your opponent's card.
  4. Winning Rounds:
    • Rock beats Scissors
    • Scissors beats Paper
    • Paper beats Rock
    • If both players use the same type, the card with higher power wins!
  5. Scoring: Win a round to earn points! The player with the most points after all cards are played wins the game.
  6. Victory: May the best strategist win! üëë

Tips:

  • Pay attention to your opponent's playing patterns
  • Save high-power cards for crucial moments
  • Balance your deck composition for better versatility
